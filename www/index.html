• Type a number in a cell to guess how many numbers you and your opponent will wind up placing in that row or column.
    • The number must be between 1 and the number of rows or columns in the grid.
    • It's illegal to have two of the same number in the same row or column.
  • If you want to stop a cell from having any number, you can type "0" or "X" to cross a cell out without placing a number in it.
  • When a row or column is filled, the player who guessed correctly will get one point for each number in the row or column.
